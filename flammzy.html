Founder & Head DJ — D'Flamz Nation

I'm DJ Flammzy, also known as The King Maker an award-winning London-based Nigerian DJ with over a decade of experience making crowds move and parties come alive.

My sound is truly global, rooted in a London movement that celebrates culture, rhythm, and connection. As a proudly Black, multicultural DJ, I bring together African, Caribbean, Indian, Spanish, and Turkish music to create high-energy sets that connect people across cultures.

Recognised as the hottest African DJ in the UK, I proudly lead the DJ crew D'FLAMZ Nation, continuing to build experiences that celebrate music, identity, and community.
