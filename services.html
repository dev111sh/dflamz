Our Services
What We Do

From single-night bookings to long-term residencies, DJ training to full event production — D'Flamz Nation delivers fire at every level.

01
DJ Booking & Management
Book any DJ from our roster or Gang of DJs network for your venue, event, or residency. We manage logistics, contracts, and performance standards end-to-end.
02
DJ Academy & Training
Our academy develops the next generation of DJs — from technical mixing fundamentals to stage presence, branding, and career development.
03
Event Production & Festivals
We produce events from concept to execution. Our BPM DJ Festival drew 5,000+ attendees in its debut — we know how to build a moment.
04
Venue Residencies
Ongoing partnerships with bars, lounges, restaurants, and hotels — delivering consistent, curated entertainment that keeps guests coming back.
05
Music Promotion
Amplify your sound. We plug into our radio and media network — City 105.1, Kiss FM Lagos, Beat FM London — to push your music where it needs to be heard.
06
Brand & Sponsorship
Partner with D'Flamz Nation to reach Lagos and London's entertainment audiences. Festival sponsorships, branded residencies, and media integration available.
