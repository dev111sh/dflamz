About
Spreading Music
Like Wild Fire

D'Flamz Nation Ltd is Nigeria's leading DJ management, training, and entertainment company — built on a single belief: that music, properly delivered, is a force of nature.

Founded and led by award-winning DJ Flammzy, D'Flamz Nation trains, develops, and manages top-tier DJs for venues, events, and residencies across Nigeria, the UK, and beyond. From intimate lounges to festival stages of 5,000+, our DJs do not just play music — they own the room.

Our Gang of DJs network extends our reach further, connecting venues with carefully vetted independent talent who carry the same standard our name is built on.
